Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the creation of something new or the improvement of existing things. It can involve new ideas, products, services, or processes.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the ability to meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the basic physical systems of a country or region, such as transportation, communication, power, and water supply.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of increasing the autonomy and agency of individuals, communities, or groups.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh envisions a future where economic growth is intertwined with environmental stewardship. We strive to harness renewable energy sources, embrace sustainable agriculture, and foster a circular economy. By prioritizing resource efficiency, promoting green technologies, and empowering communities, we aim to build a prosperous and resilient nation for generations to come."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
